Inside the coaching

What actually happens once you start.

No guessing. Here is every step, from the day you join to your weekly check ins.

From day one

Your first week, step by step.

01

You join

We share a detailed questionnaire that captures every small detail about your body, routine, history and goals.

02

Your private group

We create your personal WhatsApp group, share your welcome kit, and introduce you to your team.

03

Nutrition counselling

Your Nutrition Journey Master gets on a call to understand your current food habits and daily routine.

04

Workout counselling

Your Workout Journey Master gets on a call to understand how you train and how you move.

05

Your plan is built

The team builds your plan around everything we have learned. Gaurav personally checks every plan before it reaches you.

06

Your plan arrives

You receive it as one clear Google Sheet, with five sections built just for you.

Your plan, in one place

Five sections. Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Nutrition plan

Built around your condition, preferences and routine, updated as you progress.

Workout plan

Matched to your equipment, schedule and body, whether that is home or gym.

Lifestyle activities

The small daily habits, water, steps, sleep, that make the plan actually hold.

Supplements

Only what is genuinely useful for your body. Nothing fancy or wasteful.

Weekly tracking sheet

Where you log your weight and measurements each week, so progress is clear.

Every day

You are never doing this alone.

Your meals, checked daily

You share a photo of every meal plate. Your Nutrition Journey Master checks each one and corrects along the way, so you always know you are on track.

Your movement, tracked

You post your workout updates in the group. On the PT program, your Workout Journey Master runs your sessions live with you.

Every week

We watch the data, and adjust.

You fill your tracking sheet

Weight and body measurements, every week, so progress is visible and honest.

Your plan is adjusted

Based on how your body is actually responding, we modify the plan when it needs it.

Your weekly call

Your Nutrition Journey Master gets on a call with you every week. Gaurav stays in the group throughout, tracking everything himself.

Ready when you are

This is the level of support most plans never gave you.
