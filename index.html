The problem

Most fat loss advice was never built for your body.

Generic diet plans assume every body burns fat the same way.

PCOS and thyroid conditions do not work like that. Insulin resistance. A slower metabolism. Stubborn weight that will not move no matter how little you eat.

Most women with these conditions have been failing plans that were never built for them in the first place.

It was never about willpower. It was the wrong approach.

It takes the average woman 4.5 years and 4 different doctors to get a PCOS diagnosis. Along the way, most are told their labs are normal, or that this is just part of being a woman.

That is not your fault. That is a system that was never built to hear you.
