What's actually happening

Your body isn't broken. It's running on different instructions.

Three things quietly work against you with PCOS and thyroid. Once you can see them, the last few years start to make sense.

01 — Insulin

Fat gets stored more easily

With PCOS, your cells respond less to insulin. So your body holds onto fat, especially around the middle, and burns it more slowly. Eating even less does not fix that signal.

02 — Metabolism

You burn less for the same effort

An underactive thyroid lowers the rate your body uses energy. The same food and the same workout give you a smaller result than they give someone without it.

03 — The cycle

It feeds on itself

High insulin drives cravings and energy crashes, which lead to more storage. Left alone, the loop tightens. This is why willpower alone was never going to win.

So we fix the hormones first. The fat loss follows.
