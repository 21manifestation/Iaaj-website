About IAAJ

It was never about willpower.

I watched women suffer. I decided to fix it.

It's All About Journey was built on one idea: standard diets are made for bodies without hormonal conditions. That is why they fail women with PCOS and thyroid issues.

The founder

Gaurav Pawar

Gaurav founded It's All About Journey in 2017, and has spent 10 years in health and fitness coaching. Since then, IAAJ has coached over 5,000 clients, first from Pune, now across India and increasingly with clients internationally.

The hormonal specialisation came from a simple pattern he kept seeing: women doing everything right on paper, and still not seeing results, because the plan was never built for a body dealing with PCOS or thyroid issues in the first place. Fix the hormones first, and the results follow.
